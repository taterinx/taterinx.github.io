The Wedding Meta

Why is the ceremony itself private?
There are a few different reasons. The short answer: Privacy. It also gave us a lot of planning power.
Why are you hosting multiple events?
Our day is only as special as the people we invite, and it's unfair to expect our close friends to pay hundreds to fly out or to take too much time off work just for us, especially since we've foregone the traditional celebrations.
Why are some dates DD/MM/YY (UK) and others MM/DD/YY (USA)?
Maintaining the transatlantic peace.
